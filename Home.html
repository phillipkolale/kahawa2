About us

About Our Company 

At Kahawa Direct, we are passionate about connecting the world’s coffee drinkers with the best beans from East African farmers.

We maintain excellent relationships with our farmers and wholesalers to ensure that we only source the highest quality beans. Our farmers share our dedication to quality, adopting rigorous growing and harvesting techniques

As an East African coffee wholesaler, we take our responsibility to deliver seriously. We continually look for the best beans, perfecting our roasting procedures, and delivering great products in a timely manner.

Join us on this adventure, whether you’re a coffee shop, restaurant, or retail distributor. Experience the passion, skill, and dedication that distinguishes Kahawa Direct apart. Let’s elevate the coffee experience together.

Quality begins at the source, and we place a premium on these collaborations. Our commitment extends beyond quality. We are dedicated to sustainability and ethical practices. Supporting fair trade initiatives, environmentally friendly farming, and the well-being of coffee-growing communities is important to us.
